I finally carved out a small, permanent place online to collect the things I think
about. This site is part notebook, part portfolio: a way to keep my research,
publications, and half-formed ideas in one tidy spot.

## What you'll find here

- **Research** — the projects I am actively working on, currently centered on
  surrogate-assisted optimization for aerodynamic shape design.
- **Publications** — papers, preprints, and talks, each with links and a copyable
  BibTeX entry.
- **Blog** — short write-ups, lessons learned, and notes-to-self that might be
  useful to someone else chasing similar problems.

## Why a blog at all?

Writing forces clarity. When I try to explain *why* a Gaussian-process surrogate
helps a particle swarm converge with fewer CFD calls, I usually discover the gaps
in my own understanding. Publishing those explanations keeps me honest.

> The best way to learn something is to explain it to someone else.

More soon. Thanks for stopping by.
